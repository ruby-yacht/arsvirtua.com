Projects Reaching Into the Realm of Warcraft


Ars Virtua has established a guild which operates on both the Horde and Alliance sides of the conflict. The AV Guild caters not only to virtual artists working in world but any individual interested in art and utilizing the platform in an unorthadox manner.

The AV Mission:
Spread love, not hate. Create a new kind of art. Expand the tribe that is Ars Virtua.

- Jennifer Lau, Virtual Worlds Intern.
